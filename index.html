What PAIRA Does

PAIRA is a supportive platform designed to help couples track menstrual health together. Women can log their cycle data, while partners stay informed and offer support through custom messages and educational resources.

Mission and Values

Our mission is to foster empathy and awareness in menstrual health. We value privacy, education, and connection, providing a safe space for couples to navigate this journey together.
